          Are handbags and purses the same thing?
          While purses are comparatively smaller and used to carry only the bare essentials, handbags are normally bigger and accommodate many more items. Purses have longer straps, and clutches have shorter straps.
          Are totes similar to handbags?
          Totes are larger, strong bags normally fashioned out of cloth and are unfastened. They can accommodate more items being bigger, and are therefore more versatile.
